      What is a Freeze Token?
      Life happens — travel, illness, emergencies. A Freeze Token lets you protect your streaks for one day without completing any habits.

      How it works:
      🔹 You get 2 tokens per month — use them wisely
      🔹 Frozen days count as ✅ for streak calculations
      🔹 Your coins are not affected on frozen days
      🔹 The day shows ❄️ in your 7-day calendar
      🔹 Tokens reset when you start a new month

      When to use: Only on days you truly can't maintain your routine. Not as a shortcut — your Sankalp deserves better 🙏
